Information

We are thrilled to invite you to celebrate with us at Vineyard Vista Gardens in beautiful Napa Valley. Our ceremony will take place outdoors among the rolling vineyards, followed by cocktails and reception under the stars. The venue offers stunning views of the valley and provides the perfect backdrop for our special day.

Please plan to arrive by 3:45 PM for our 4:00 PM ceremony. The celebration will continue with cocktail hour immediately following the ceremony, dinner service beginning at 7:00 PM, and dancing until 11:00 PM. We encourage cocktail attire and comfortable shoes for walking on grass paths throughout the vineyard grounds.

Complimentary parking will be available on-site, and we'll have shuttles running from nearby hotels for your convenience. The weather in Napa Valley in June is typically warm during the day with cooler evenings, so please bring a light jacket or wrap for the evening celebration.
